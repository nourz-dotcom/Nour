Breakfast Recipes
🥚
Egg & Spinach Toastie
5 min · air fryer
Ingredients:
2 eggs · handful of spinach · 1 slice cheese · 2 slices sourdough

Method:
Scramble eggs in a pan with wilted spinach. Place on toasted sourdough. Top with cheese slice. Air fryer 2–3 min until melted.

High protein, iron from spinach, satisfying and warm.
🫙
Turkey & Labneh
3 min · no cook
Ingredients:
80–100g turkey slices · 1 tbsp labneh · sliced cucumber · dried mint · 2 slices sourdough

Method:
Toast bread. Spread labneh. Layer turkey and cucumber. Sprinkle dried mint. Done.

No cooking. Fresh and light. Perfect when you have no energy.
🍗
Chicken Pesto Toastie
4 min · air fryer
Ingredients:
Leftover or rotisserie chicken · 1 tsp pesto · sliced tomato · 2 slices sourdough

Method:
Toast bread. Spread pesto on both slices. Layer chicken and tomato. Air fryer 3 min until warm and slightly crispy.

Uses leftover chicken. Feels like a proper café sandwich.
🍳
Fried Egg & Cheese
5 min · one pan
Ingredients:
2 eggs · 1 slice cheese · sliced tomato · 2 slices sourdough · tiny bit of butter

Method:
Fry eggs in a tiny bit of butter. Toast bread. Place egg on toast, top with cheese, let it melt from the heat of the egg. Add tomato slices.

The simplest comfort breakfast. Never gets old.
🥑
Turkey & Avocado Wrap
3 min · no cook
Ingredients:
80–100g turkey slices · ½ avocado mashed · squeeze of lemon · salt · 1 wrap or pita

Method:
Warm the wrap 30 seconds in microwave. Spread mashed avocado. Layer turkey. Squeeze lemon, add salt. Roll and eat.

Creamy, satisfying, no cooking at all.
Lunch Recipes
🍗
Air Fryer Chicken & Roasted Veg
20 min · air fryer
Ingredients:
120–150g chicken breast · zucchini · carrot · olive oil · salt · cumin or paprika · side of rice or bread

Method:
Slice chicken and veg. Toss in olive oil and spices. Air fryer 180°C for 16–18 min, shaking halfway. Serve with rice or a slice of sourdough.

One basket, almost no cleanup. Warm and satisfying.
🥗
Turkey & Potato Plate
15 min · microwave + airfryer
Ingredients:
80–100g turkey slices · 150–200g potato · cucumber · tomato · labneh or sauce of choice

Method:
Microwave potato until soft (5–7 min), then air fryer 5 min for crispy skin. Serve turkey slices alongside with raw veg and a dollop of labneh.

High volume, very filling, minimal effort.
🐟
Salmon with Spinach & Rice
18 min · one pan
Ingredients:
120–150g salmon fillet · handful of spinach · 100g cooked rice · lemon · olive oil · salt

Method:
Pan-fry salmon in a little olive oil, 4 min each side. Wilt spinach in the same pan for 1 min. Serve over rice with a squeeze of lemon.

Omega-3s, anti-inflammatory, feels like a restaurant plate.
Dinner Recipes
🥩
Steak & Air Fryer Carrots
20 min · air fryer
Ingredients:
120–150g steak · carrots sliced · olive oil · salt · pepper · garlic powder

Method:
Toss carrots in olive oil and seasoning. Air fryer 15 min at 190°C. Pan-sear steak 2–3 min each side. Rest 2 min before cutting. Serve together.

High protein, naturally sweet carrots, no stress.
🍲
One-Pan Chicken & Zucchini
20 min · one pan
Ingredients:
120–150g chicken breast · zucchini · tomatoes · garlic · olive oil · dried herbs · pita or sourdough

Method:
Dice chicken and zucchini. Sauté garlic in olive oil 1 min. Add chicken, cook 5 min. Add zucchini and tomatoes, cook 8 min until soft. Season. Serve with bread to scoop.

Comforting, warm, feels like home cooking. One pan.
🥚
Shakshuka-Style Eggs
15 min · one pan
Ingredients:
3 eggs · tinned or fresh tomatoes · spinach · garlic · olive oil · cumin · paprika · sourdough to serve

Method:
Sauté garlic in olive oil. Add tomatoes and spices, simmer 5 min. Add spinach, wilt. Make wells, crack eggs in. Cover and cook 5–7 min until whites set. Serve with bread.

Warming, deeply satisfying, looks beautiful in the pan.
